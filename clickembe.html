Click Embellish

Project information

  • Category: Web Development
  • Project date: 2021 (first Project)
  • Project URL: Click Embellish

"Click Embellish" is an innovative platform that combines social media features with event management. Users can create accounts, build their profiles, and upload photos to share their experiences. Event management companies can also create profiles, allowing them to showcase their work and demos to potential clients. This dual-purpose platform enhances both social interaction and professional visibility in the event management industry.
